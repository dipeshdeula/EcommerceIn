Privacy Policy for InstantMart

Last updated: August 4, 2025

Effective Date: August 4, 2025

Important: This privacy policy describes how InstantMart collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our e-commerce platform and Google Sign-In services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Google Sign-In Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect the following information with your explicit consent:

  • Email Address: Used for account identification and communication
  • Full Name: Used for personalizing your experience and order processing
  • Profile Picture: Used to enhance your user profile (optional)
  • Google Account ID: Used for secure authentication
  • Account Verification Status: To ensure account security

1.2 E-commerce Information

We collect information necessary for providing our e-commerce services:

  • Order Information: Products purchased, quantities, prices, order history
  • Shipping Information: Delivery addresses, contact numbers
  • Payment Information: Payment method preferences (we do not store payment card details)
  • Communication: Customer service interactions, reviews, feedback
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device identifiers for security
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, search queries, interaction patterns

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google Services: Information from Google APIs as authorized by you
  • Payment Processors: Transaction status from Esewa, Khalti, and other payment providers
  • Delivery Partners: Delivery status and tracking information

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Uses

  • Account Management: Create, maintain, and secure your account
  • Order Processing: Process purchases, manage inventory, handle returns
  • Communication: Send order confirmations, updates, and customer support
  • Payment Processing: Facilitate secure transactions through our payment partners
  • Delivery Services: Coordinate product delivery and tracking

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our platform
  • Personalize product recommendations
  • Enhance user experience and interface design
  • Develop new features and services

2.3 Legal and Security Uses

  • Comply with legal obligations and regulations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Enforce our terms of service

3. Google Sign-In Compliance

Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance: Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3.1 Limited Use Requirements

  • We only request access to Google user data that is necessary for our e-commerce functionality
  • We do not use Google user data for serving advertisements
  • We do not allow humans to read Google user data unless required for security purposes or compliance
  • We do not transfer Google user data to third parties except as necessary for our e-commerce services

3.2 Data Minimization

We only access and store the minimum amount of Google user data necessary to provide our services:

  • Basic profile information for account creation
  • Email address for communication and account verification
  • No access to sensitive data like contacts, calendar, or drive files

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Service Providers

We share your information with trusted third parties who assist in operating our platform:

  • Payment Processors: Esewa, Khalti, and other payment providers for transaction processing
  • Delivery Partners: For order fulfillment and tracking
  • Cloud Services: For hosting and data storage (with appropriate security measures)
  • Analytics Providers: For understanding user behavior (anonymized data only)

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal process or government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or security threats
  • Enforce our terms of service

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to the same privacy protections.

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication for our systems
  • Regular Audits: Regular security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Data Backup: Secure backup and disaster recovery procedures
  • Employee Training: Regular privacy and security training for our staff

5.2 Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Order History: Retained for 7 years for legal and tax purposes
  • Google Data: Retained only as long as necessary for service provision
  • Marketing Data: Retained until you opt-out or request deletion

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • Account Access: View and update your account information anytime
  • Data Download: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Data Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Account Deletion: Delete your account and associated data

6.2 Google Account Management

  • Revoke Google Sign-In permissions through your Google Account settings
  • Manage what information Google shares with InstantMart
  • Disconnect your Google account from InstantMart at any time

6.3 Communication Preferences

  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Choose your notification preferences
  • Update your communication channels

7. Cookies and Tracking

7.1 Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic platform functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how you use our site
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Authentication Cookies: Keep you signed in securely

7.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Nepal. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international transfers, including:

  • Adequate data protection agreements
  • Compliance with applicable data protection laws
  • Secure data transfer protocols

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete such information promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Sending an email notification to your registered email address
  • Posting a notice on our platform
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
